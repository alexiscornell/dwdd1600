Trail Information

At Ridge Rock Bike Haven, we take pride in offering trails that cater to every level of mountain biking expertise. From the gentle curves of our beginner-friendly paths to the exhilarating challenges of our advanced downhill routes, there's something for everyone. Our diverse range of trails ensures that whether you're just starting out or are a seasoned pro seeking adrenaline-pumping thrills, you'll find a ride that perfectly matches your skill level. Each trail is meticulously designed to provide a unique experience, making Ridge Rock a destination where bikers of all abilities can find their perfect adventure.

Trail Ettiquette

At Ridge Rock Bike Haven, we’re committed to maintaining a safe and enjoyable environment for all riders. We kindly ask that every visitor practice good trail etiquette by yielding to fellow bikers, respecting trail signs and closures, and leaving no trace of their visit. By following these simple guidelines, you help preserve the trails for everyone and ensure a positive experience for all. Let's work together to keep Ridge Rock a welcoming and thrilling destination for mountain biking enthusiasts of every skill level
